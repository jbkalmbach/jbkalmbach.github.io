Bryce Kalmbach

I am a PhD candidate in Physics at the University of Washington interested in developing the tools to study large, next generation datasets in astronomy. I currently work on using machine learning methods to estimate galaxy spectra from colors and the applications of this method. I am also working on using probabilistic methods and GPUs to search for moving objects in astronomical images. Additionally, I contribute to simulation efforts in the LSST Dark Energy Science Collaboration.

This space is new and currently under development. Please keep an eye out for more soon!
